About SMCC

Saint Michael College of Caraga (SMCC), founded in 1948 by the Missionaries of the Sacred Heart (MSC) fathers, is a private Roman Catholic institution located in Nasipit, Agusan del Norte, Philippines. Initially named Saint Michael’s Institute, it was renamed in 2002 to reflect its expanded academic offerings and dedication to higher education. Operating under the Roman Catholic Diocese of Butuan, SMCC provides a holistic educational experience from basic education to higher education, fostering spiritual formation, academic excellence, and leadership development.

The Basic Education Department includes programs from Preschool to Senior High School, offering strands like STEM, ABM, HUMSS, GAS, Maritime, Home Economics, and Information Technology for Grades 11 and 12. Its College Department features various colleges offering diverse programs, such as Teacher Education, Business Administration, Arts and Sciences, Computer Studies, Criminology, and Tourism Management, ensuring a broad spectrum of opportunities for students to pursue their aspirations.

Committed to its vision of becoming a university by 2035, SMCC emphasizes spiritual growth, academic excellence, and service to the community. It aims to transform lives by fostering a culture of learning that prepares students to excel in their fields while nurturing their faith and values. SMCC also extends its educational mission through initiatives like DXSM 89.5 Kabayaning Michaelinian FM, a radio station promoting Catholic education, and its official publication, the Michaelinian Mirror, which keeps the community informed about institutional updates.

Through its comprehensive programs and unwavering commitment to its mission, Saint Michael College of Caraga continues to be a vital force in the educational and spiritual development of the Caraga region.

Courrse Offered

Saint Michael College of Caraga proudly offers a range of excellent academic programs designed to shape future leaders and professionals. Here are three of its standout courses:

Bachelor of Science in Business Administration (BSBA) – This program equips students with a solid foundation in financial management, entrepreneurship, and strategic business planning. Graduates are prepared to excel in corporate settings, start their own ventures, or pursue leadership roles in various industries.

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) – This course focuses on the design, development, and optimization of software and systems. Students gain hands-on experience in programming, database management, and emerging technologies, making them highly competitive in the ever-evolving tech industry.

Bachelor of Science in Criminology (BSCrim) – Perfect for those with a passion for law enforcement and public safety, this program trains students in criminal investigation, forensic science, and the justice system. Graduates often pursue careers as police officers, forensic analysts, or criminologists dedicated to upholding peace and security.

Services

Saint Michael College of Caraga (SMCC) offers a wide range of services to support its students, faculty, and the community. The institution provides comprehensive student services, including counseling, career guidance, and academic advising to help students navigate their educational journey. Scholarship programs are also available to assist deserving students in achieving their academic goals. The school boasts a well-equipped library with both digital and physical resources, offering access to books, journals, and online databases to support research and continuous learning. As a Catholic institution, SMCC emphasizes faith formation through religious services, retreats, and community outreach programs organized by the Campus Ministry Office, fostering spiritual growth and service-oriented values.

In addition to academic and spiritual support, SMCC ensures the well-being of its students through medical and dental services, providing basic healthcare and first aid assistance. The school also promotes holistic development by encouraging students to participate in various organizations, sports teams, and extracurricular activities that enhance leadership, teamwork, and social skills. With the integration of technology in education, SMCC offers e-learning services, online enrollment, and IT support to help students adapt to digital learning tools. To maintain a safe and secure learning environment, the institution implements campus safety policies, employs security personnel, and has emergency response systems in place. Through these services, SMCC remains committed to providing a high-quality education while ensuring the overall well-being and success of its students.
